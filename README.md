# Eunpyo Lee

I am an independent researcher interested in understanding and improving deep learning systems.

My research interests include **model interpretability**, **neural network quantization**, **efficient deep learning**, and **optical character recognition**.

- Neural Network Quantization & Model Compression

---

## Currently Working On

- **Circuit Preservation under Quantization**  
  Measuring whether quantized language models preserve or rewire internal circuits.

- **Gradient Signals in Mixed-Precision Quantization**  
  Studying what gradient-based signals actually measure in mixed-precision quantization.

- **Recall-first Candidate Proposal for Online HMER**  
  Lightweight candidate proposal for online handwritten mathematical expression recognition.

- **Typin**  
  Local-first knowledge graph note-taking system with RAG and AI assistance.
